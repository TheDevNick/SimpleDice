    How To Play

  1. Each turn, a player rolls a dice as many times as they want. Each result get added to their current score.
  2. If player rolls a 1, the overall score gets lost. After that, it's the next player's turn.
  3. The player can choose to 'Hold', which means that the current score gets added to their overall score. After that, it's the next player's turn.
  4. The 1st player to 30 wins if there is no winning score set.
